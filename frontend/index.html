Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of food do you offer?

We offer a variety of delicious meals including local dishes like moimoi, peper soup, and different types of drinks with Jollof, fried rice, shawama, and many more.

Are your meals freshly prepared?

Absolutely! All our meals are prepared fresh daily using high-quality ingredients.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us or using our WhatsApp number listed on the Contact page. Online ordering will be available soon.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we deliver to selected areas. Delivery times and charges may vary depending on your location.

What is the estimated delivery time?

Delivery typically takes between 30–60 minutes depending on your location and order size.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, and mobile payment (e.g., Opay, PalmPay). Soon, we will enable online card payments.
