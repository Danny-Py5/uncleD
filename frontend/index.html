Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get started?

Click on the Menu Icon on the header. From the dropdown menu, click on the Sign Up button. You would be redirected to the Sign-up page. Input your email and password. click on the submit button to create an account for you

You would be redirected to the Login page to verify your account from the link that would be sent to your provided email address

How does it work?

Simply browse our menu, add your favorite dishes to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Once your order is confirmed, our team will prepare your meal and deliver it to your location.

Where do you deliver?

We currently deliver to Ilu-Alaye, Oniparaga, Rubber Estate and Araromi-Obu. If your area is not listed, stay tuned as we’re expanding our delivery zones.

How do I place an order?

Browse our menu, select the dishes you want, and click on "Add to Cart." When you're ready, go to your cart and proceed with payment to confirm your order.

Can I schedule an order for later?

Absolutely! You can select a delivery time during the checkout process to have your food delivered at a later time.

What happens if my order is delayed?

We strive to deliver on time, but delays may occur due to traffic or other unforeseen circumstances. We’ll notify you immediately if there’s a delay.
